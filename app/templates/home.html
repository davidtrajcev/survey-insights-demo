Demo overview

This demo shows how an internal survey platform can collect employee feedback, preserve anonymity, respect manager hierarchy, and show trends over time.

Org-aware reporting

Managers will see their own team and all teams below them in the org tree.

Historical org snapshots

Survey results will stay connected to the org structure from the time of the survey.

Anonymity threshold

Teams with fewer than 4 respondents will be hidden in the manager dashboard.

Trends over time

Results will show movement across annual, half-year, and pulse survey cycles.

MVP assumptions

  • Company SSO is used for authentication, eligibility, and duplicate-submission prevention.
  • Responses are stored separately from employee identity and are only linked to the survey cycle and org snapshot unit.
  • Every survey cycle uses a frozen org snapshot so org changes do not rewrite historical results.
  • Manager dashboards consume privacy-safe aggregates, not raw responses.
  • Teams with fewer than 4 respondents are hidden; production would add stronger secondary suppression for inference risks.
